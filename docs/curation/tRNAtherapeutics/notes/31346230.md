## Linked Construct Evidence Checked

- The paper states that it introduced a previously described evolved bacterial YRS/suppressor tRNA^Tyr_CUA pair from `E. coli` into `T. brucei`, citing Chatterjee et al. 2013, PMID 23818609, DOI 10.1073/pnas.1309584110.
- PMID 23818609 full text and supplement were downloaded from PMC after resolving the PMC POW download gate:
  - `field-curation-workdir/full_tRNAtherapeutics/papers/23818609/23818609_PMC3718144.html`
  - `field-curation-workdir/full_tRNAtherapeutics/papers/23818609/23818609_PMC3718144.pdf`
  - `field-curation-workdir/full_tRNAtherapeutics/supplementary/23818609/1309584110_pnas.201309584SI.pdf`
  - `field-curation-workdir/full_tRNAtherapeutics/supplementary/23818609/supp_110_29_11803__index.html`
- Method details in PMID 31346230 are more specific than the Results section: `pAcBac2.tR4-OMeYRS/GFP*` from Addgene supplied the oMeYRS and nsGFP coding sequences. The tRNA construct itself was synthesized commercially by Genescript and contained the `E. coli` tRNA^Tyr_CUA gene plus 50 nt upstream and 30 nt downstream flanking sequence from a `T. brucei` tRNA^Leu gene.
- PMID 31346230 explicitly states that the genetically encoded 3' acceptor `CCA` present in the `E. coli` tRNA^Tyr_CUA gene was omitted in the synthesized construct. Therefore, if this row is updated, the stored mature/body sequence should not include the encoded terminal `CCA` unless a separate storage convention requires it.
- PMID 23818609 Fig. 2A lists Ec and Bacillus stearothermophilus Tyr tRNA_CUA sequences in the Addgene-source baculovirus cassette. For PMID 31346230, only the `E. coli` tRNA is relevant.
- Addgene sequence page for plasmid #50831 exposes sequence visualization data for Addgene full sequence `193569`. The GenBank download URL requires login/returns unavailable anonymously, but `sequence-lines.json` was accessible and was saved locally:
  - `field-curation-workdir/full_tRNAtherapeutics/plasmids/addgene_50831/addgene-plasmid-50831-sequence-193569-sequence-lines.json`
  - `field-curation-workdir/full_tRNAtherapeutics/plasmids/addgene_50831/addgene-plasmid-50831-sequence-193569-features.json`
- Reconstructing the top strand from `sequence-lines.json` gives plasmid length 11408 bp. The `E. coli` Tyr(CUA) tRNA sequence is present at 1-based position 7491 with `...GACTCTAAATC...`; `...GACTGTAAATC...` is absent. This resolves the CR19 figure ambiguity in favor of `CUA`.
- Sequence supported for this row, after applying the PMID 31346230 statement that encoded terminal `CCA` was omitted: `GGUGGGGUUCCCGAGCGGCCAAAGGGAGCAGACUCUAAAUCUGCCGUCACAGACUUCGAAGGUUCGAAUCCUUCCCCCACCA` (82 nt).
