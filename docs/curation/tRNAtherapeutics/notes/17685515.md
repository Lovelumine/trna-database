## Sequence Evidence

- Kwon 2007 uses `pREP4_ytRNAPhe_UG` to express mutant yeast amber suppressor `ytRNAPheCUA_UG`.
- The Caltech thesis construction chapter states that `ytRNAPheCUA` was inserted into pREP4 as described by Furter, and that `ytRNAPheCUA_30U40G` (`ytRNAPheCUA_UG`) was generated with primers `UG_f` and `UG_r`.
- The same chapter states that Kwon's `ytRNAPheCUA` is the G37A mutant of `ytRNAPheCUA_WT`; `ytRNAPheCUA_UG` then adds the 30U-40G wobble pair.
- Furter 1998 directly lists the yeast amber suppressor tRNA gene sequence and states that the G 3' to the anticodon was replaced by A (G37A).
- Kwon 2006 directly states that `ytRNAPheCUA_UG` was constructed from `ytRNAPheCUA` by introducing mutations at positions 30 and 40, giving the 30U-40G wobble pair.
- Kwon 2006 states that in vitro transcription produced 76-mer tRNA transcripts.
- Kwon's PCR primer for in vitro transcription starts the tRNA sequence as `GCGGACTTAGCTC`, matching the S. cerevisiae S288C tRNA-Phe(GAA) tRNA core rather than GenBank M10263/PDB 1EHZ (`GCGGATTT...`).
- Proposed origin RNA sequence with encoded 3' CCA: `GCGGACUUAGCUCAGUUGGGAGAGCGCCAGACUGAAGAUCUGGAGGUCCUGUGUUCGAUCCACAGAGUUCGCACCA`.
- Proposed suppressor RNA sequence with encoded 3' CCA: `GCGGACUUAGCUCAGUUGGGAGAGCGCCAUACUCUAAAUGUGGAGGUCCUGUGUUCGAUCCACAGAGUUCGCACCA`.
- Mutations in the derived suppressor sequence relative to the S288C tRNA-Phe(GAA) reference are: anticodon GAA->CUA at positions 34-36, G37A, G30U, and C40G.
- The reverse complement of Kwon's `UG_f` primer exactly matches the proposed suppressor DNA sequence segment, supporting the 30U/40G and G37A interpretation.
